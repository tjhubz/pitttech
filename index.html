Pitt Account Settings

Do you use a different name than your legal name? Would you like to set a nickname for yourself? It can all be done in the Pitt Account management portal! Any preferences set here will update your information across any university affiliated service, such as the roster of students given to your professor.

Your Email

Your Pitt email is one of the most important parts of your computing account, and it's essential that you check it frequently to ensure you don't miss important updates. Your email is provided by Microsoft Outlook, but that doesn't mean you have to use their app! You can sign into your university email on any email app that you choose.
